The Len'en mod is a mod based on the Bullet Hell series "Len'en Project".
It adds 13 accessories, 16 weapons, two NPCs and a special tile.
Please report any bugs or suggestions in this mod's workshop page comment section or make a new Discussion.

Ver 0.2.10.0
+ Increased the amount of spirits drained from the player after a hit near the Harujion.
+ Increased the spirit item collection hitbox.
+ Spirit items can now be picked up regardless of the player’s inventory space.
+ Doubled the drop rate of fairy related items, from 1 in 60 to 1 in 30 for all of them except Sake.
+ Most danmaku bullets now have damage penetration.
+ Added the Power Rod Bat summoner weapon and Senri Priest Hat accessory.
	* Bosses now drop Boss Ofuda of their respective type, used in conjunction with the Senri Priest Hat.
	* Currently, only non-event vanilla bosses have an Ofuda, all other bosses will drop the default ofuda.
+ Added Big Hat vanity item, with an eyeless variant.
+ The Lumen Ball now scales with summon damage.
+ Flashbomb immunity now extends to all forms of damage, including traps and bosses.
+ Projectile max thrill now increases with every 35 damage instead of every 50.
+ Projectile thrill cooldown is now 10 frames instead of 15.
+ Increased the minimum thrill range from 50 to 60.
- Greatly reduced the Harujion’s life draining effects, reduced its range in half, and reduced the amount of tiles affected on every absorption attempt.
- Reduced Dimensional Fragment spawn chance from falling stars from 1 in 40 to 1 in 60.
* Added a config option to block the Harujion from appearing and disable its related effects, turned off by default. It will not destroy or modify any already existing Harujion tiles.
* Added a config option to return the severity of the Harujion’s effects to how they were before this update, turned off by default.
* Removed debug text upon creating a Flashbomb projectile.
* Set the Spirit item’s collection range to 450 pixels, doubled when having the Harujion equipped.
* Modified danmaku bullets’ collision detection to be more accurate to its sprite.
* Slightly changed the player targeting system.
